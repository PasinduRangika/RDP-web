How does Caprome support educational settings?
Caprome is designed with education in mind. It allows teachers to monitor student screens in real-time, track engagement, and provide feedback, all within a safe and secure platform. Caprome also enables parents to stay informed and promotes responsible screen usage among students, making it an invaluable tool for digital classrooms.
Can I monitor students in real-time?
Yes! Caprome offers real-time screen monitoring, allowing teachers and administrators to observe student activities instantly. This feature helps ensure that students stay focused during lessons and can quickly intervene if a student becomes distracted.
Is Caprome compatible with multiple devices?
Absolutely. Caprome is optimized for use on Chromebooks and is also compatible with other devices through our web-based application. This flexibility makes it easy to implement Caprome in both traditional classrooms and remote learning environments.
How secure is my data with Caprome?
Security is our top priority. Caprome uses advanced encryption to protect your data, and we follow strict access control measures to ensure that only authorized users can access sensitive information. Our platform is also compliant with educational data protection regulations, giving you peace of mind.
How do I get started with a Caprome demo?
Getting started is easy! Simply click the "Request Demo" button on our website, and our team will reach out to schedule a personalized demo. We’ll guide you through Caprome’s features and answer any questions you have about implementing it in your educational environment.
